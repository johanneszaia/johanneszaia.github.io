Johannes Zaia

Welcome! I am a doctoral researcher at the Graduate School of Decision Sciences at University of Konstanz and associated with the Chair of Corporate Finance.

My research focuses on Corporate Finance and Governance with a focus on top management compensation and the composition of top management teams. Moreover, I am regularly teaching tutorials for finance courses like Advanced Corporate Finance, Behavioral Finance, and Bank Management in the B.Sc. and M.Sc. programs in Economics.
